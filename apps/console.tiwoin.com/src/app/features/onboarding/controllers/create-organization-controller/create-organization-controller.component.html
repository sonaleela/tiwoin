FAQ

What is the role of an organization in Tiwoin's onboarding process?

In Tiwoin, an organization refers to the overarching entity that encompasses your business or company. It serves as the foundation for managing and organizing your workforce. Users can be invited to join an organization, and all employees are added at the organization level, enabling centralized administration and coordination.
